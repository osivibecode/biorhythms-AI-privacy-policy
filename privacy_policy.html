Privacy Policy for Biorhythms AI

Effective Date: 12/20/2025

Thank you for using Biorhythms AI ("the App"). This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to your privacy and aim to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve the App.

  • Date of Birth: The App requires your date of birth to calculate your personal biorhythm cycles. This information is stored securely and exclusively on your local device. It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Calculated Biorhythm Data: To provide you with personalized AI-driven recommendations, the App sends your calculated, non-personal biorhythm values (e.g., "Physical: 0.85, Emotional: -0.23") to a third-party AI service (Google's AI). Your date of birth is not included in this data transmission.
  • Advertising Data: The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and other data to serve personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google handles data in its Ads Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To calculate and display your biorhythm cycles.
  • To provide personalized daily recommendations through our AI feature.
  • To display advertisements that support the App's development.
  • To enable and manage app functionalities like notifications and language preferences.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in our App:

  • Google AI (Gemini): Used to generate personalized recommendations based on your biorhythm data.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Your Date of Birth is stored only on your device, giving you full control. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect the information transmitted for AI recommendations, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete it.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or on our store listing. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
