Privacy Policy for Biorhythms AI

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Biorhythms AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve the App.

A. Data You Provide

  • Date of Birth: Required to calculate your physiological, emotional, and intellectual biorhythm cycles. This is stored securely on your local device.
  • Natal Chart Data: To provide precise astrological interpretations (Natal Chart), we may collect your exact Time of Birth and Birth Location (City, Latitude, and Longitude). This data is stored on your device and only shared with AI services if you explicitly request a chart interpretation.
  • Profile Names: Used to distinguish between saved profiles (e.g., "Me", "Partner").

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Environmental Data: We retrieve current environmental indices such as the Kp-index, magnetic field intensity, solar flux, and barometric pressure to provide a more holistic wellness analysis.
  • Advertising Data: The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and other data to serve personalized ads. You can learn more via Google's Ads Policy.

2. Third-Party AI Services

To provide personalized daily advice, compatibility analysis, and Natal Chart interpretations, Biorhythms AI uses third-party AI models.

Sharing is strictly Opt-In. No data is shared with AI services unless you explicitly enable AI features or request a Natal Chart interpretation.

Shared Data:

When AI features are enabled, the following data may be shared with our AI partners:

  • Calculated biorhythm percentages.
  • Your approximate age (calculated from your Year of Birth).
  • For Natal Charts: Your Date of Birth, Time of Birth, and approximate coordinates (Latitude/Longitude).
  • For Comparison Mode: The first name and Date of Birth of the profiles being compared.
  • Current environmental indices (Kp-index, pressure, etc.).
  • Your preferred application language.

AI Partners:

  • Google Gemini: Used for generating personalized wellness insights and astrological interpretations.
  • OpenRouter: Used as a service provider for various AI models.

These partners are prohibited from using this data for any purpose other than providing the requested AI generation for the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Calculate and display your biorhythm cycles and astrological natal chart.
  • Provide personalized daily recommendations and interpretations through our AI features.
  • Retrieve environmental data for wellness analysis.
  • Display advertisements that support the App's development.
  • Manage app functionalities like notifications and language preferences.

4. Tracking and Advertising

Biorhythms AI may use industry-standard identifiers (such as the IDFA on iOS) to provide personalized advertising through Google AdMob. This usage is strictly subject to your explicit consent via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline, your data will not be used for tracking across other apps or websites.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Sensitive profile information like your Date of Birth and natal details are stored primarily on your device. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect information transmitted for AI recommendations, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete it.

7. Your Choices & Opt-Out

  • Tracking: You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device's Privacy settings.
  • AI Consent: You can revoke AI data sharing consent at any time in the app Settings. Revoking consent will immediately delete cached AI recommendations from your device.
  • Data Erasure: You can clear all app data at any time through the "Clear Data" option in Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
